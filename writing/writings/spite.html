In Spite of it All

You are made up of your misconceptions.
You want to be different than the last generation.
But have you examined your rebellion?

This is how it seems to me:
You take the bad of your parents' generation
And ignore all the lessons they have learned.

This is how it is:
You add to these mistakes
More and sometimes worse mistakes of your own.

Why do you spite the good intentions of others?
Why do you spite your own intelligence?
Why do you spite your own future?
