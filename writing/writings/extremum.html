--- layout: poetry title: Extremum ---

On a cold day, you'll stand, basking in a patch of sunlight.
On a hot day, you'll take refuge in the shade.

But you'll play in the snow, and tan on the beach.
Unless you're from Alaska, or live in Florida.

Are the only ones who are content the ones who never leave their 75 degree homes?
Do heating and air conditioning make you happy?
Or do you alternately search for patches of sun and spots of shade?
